## Risk flags
- **Actuator (riskiest spec):** A single Amazon listing (B0D91XZ167) genuinely satisfies **all four** hard requirements at once — potentiometer position feedback, IP66, 100mm stroke option, and force well above 150N. B0FLCBKKCH is a cleaner-force (1500N) feedback+IP66 alternative. The named-brand Progressive Automations PA-14P-class unit (B081CW4D1Z) has feedback but is **50mm stroke and not IP66**.
- **Actuator on eBay is the weak spot:** no single eBay listing combined 100mm + feedback + IP66. The only concrete feedback listing found is 50mm/IP-unrated (itm/355798853071); the 100mm eBay inventory is feedback-less PA-04. Buy the actuators on Amazon.
- The 5A "adjustable" buck is LM2596-based = **3A max**, not 5A — swap to XL4015 if 5A adjustable is required.
