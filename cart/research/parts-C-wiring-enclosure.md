## Recommended build spec (what a builder actually needs)

- **Wire gauge/length:** 10 AWG tinned duplex red/black for the main battery→fuse-block feed — **~10–15 ft** is plenty (keep the high-current run short). 14 AWG tinned duplex for drive/blade-motor and actuator runs — **~25–50 ft** (16 AWG only for sub-5A loads). 22 AWG 6-color tinned stranded kit for all FC/Pi signal & sensor lines — one 6×26ft kit covers a full build.
- **Connectors/counts:** XT60 — **3–5 pairs** (battery main, motor controller, plus spares). Heat-shrink terminal assortment — one **~540pc marine kit** covers ring/spade/butt for the whole harness. Fuses: **1 central 12-way fuse block w/ ground bus** at the battery, plus **4–6 waterproof inline holders** for branch circuits; stock an **ATC/ATO fuse assortment 2–40A**.
- **Enclosure/sealing:** one **200×150×100mm IP67 ABS box** for electronics; **one 50pc mixed PG7/PG9/PG11+ gland kit** (covers the requested PG9); **MG Chemicals 422B** on all PCBs + **Permatex dielectric grease** in every gland/connector.
- **Hardware:** **304 stainless** M3/M4/M5 assortment (not zinc — it's outdoors); **M3 + M4 brass heat-set inserts** for 3D-printed mounts; one **1m 20mm-OD 6063 aluminium tube** for masts (buy 1m on eBay/Online Metals — Amazon mostly sells 200–300mm only); plus split loom (1/4–1/2"), UV-rated zip ties, 1" ratchet straps, and adhesive rubber feet.
- **Caveat:** every price is unverified in-session — confirm at each URL before buying.
