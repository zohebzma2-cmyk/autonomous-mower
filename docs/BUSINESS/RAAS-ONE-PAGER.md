large commercial campuses) at a fixed monthly rate, operator on site, hardware e-stop in hand.

**Why us / why now:** RTK autonomy on a real 615 lb commercial deck (52"), not a robot toy —
the same acre-class productivity a crew delivers, with cm-accurate stripes, no-rut headland
turns, geofenced to the property line, every pass logged (XTE + coverage map as a deliverable).

**The pilot:** one site, 90 days. Weeks 1–2 supervised mapping + teach runs (blades off).
Weeks 3–12 supervised autonomous mowing. Deliverables: per-visit coverage report, incident
log (target: zero), cost-per-acre comparison vs current contract.
